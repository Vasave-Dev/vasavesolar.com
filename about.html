About Vasave Solar

The Project



Vasave Solar is a certified renewable energy generation company established in 2012. Our mission is to make renewable energy more resourceful, accessible, and reliable form of energy. We have successfully initiated our small scale solar park project and planning to venture into wind energy in the coming future.
We at Vasave are collaborating with Paschim Gujarat Vij Company Limited (PGVCL), Gujarat Energy Transmission Corporation Limited (GETCO), and Gujarat Energy Development Agency (GEDA) under the Development of Small Scale Distributed Solar Projects- 2019 policy to generate and transmit power to the closest distribution station. PGVCL has set a target of installing 1500 solar-powered irrigation pumping systems in saurashtra Kutch region before end of this financial year. “"The thought is to offer clean energy and diminish pendency of utilizations for water system power association.”
The company has received approval to set up a 0.5MW solar plant in a 2acre land located in Metoda, Rajkot; for which the operations will be carried out from Vajadi (Kansari) S/s GETCO sub-station. We have partnered with Onix Group (1987) for installation, operation and maintenance of the project. Once completed, the project has a lifetime of 25years.
